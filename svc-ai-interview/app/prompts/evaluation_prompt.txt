ADDITIONAL METRICS

- adaptability: How well did the candidate handle follow-up questions and unexpected topics?

RESUME VERIFICATION

---
PROCTORING FACTOR

- Multiple long tab-away events during technical questions are more concerning

---
AI RECOMMENDATION

Based on total score and qualitative assessment:
- "Hire" — score >= 80 AND strong overall impression, no red flags
- "Manual Review" — score 60-79, or mixed signals, or notable concerns
- "Do Not Recommend" — score < 60, or clear red flags, or major resume inconsistencies

RED FLAGS

- Inability to discuss resume projects in depth
- Inconsistencies between resume claims and demonstrated knowledge
- Poor communication or inability to articulate thoughts
- Signs of external help during the interview (very long pauses followed by perfect answers)
- Lack of relevant domain knowledge for claimed experience level
